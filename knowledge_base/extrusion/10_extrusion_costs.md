# Extrusion Cost Drivers

Primary cost factors:

- Die complexity
- Circumscribing circle diameter
- Alloy choice
- Secondary processing

Extrusion economical when:

- Constant cross-section
- Medium/high volume
- Long profiles

## Manufacturing Implications

- Tooling amortization critical
- Hollow dies slower and costlier

## Agent Heuristics

- Large profile CCD → cost increase
- Complex hollow die → production slowdown
- Small batch + complex die → cost risk HIGH
